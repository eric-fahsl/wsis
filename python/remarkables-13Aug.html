Last Update
Monday 13 August, 16:51
Status
Open
Last Fall
5cm, 13 Aug 2012
Road
Open
The road is clear and gritted. All vehicles please carry chains and drive with care maintaining two way access at all times. Please look out for heavy machinery working on the road today.
Snow
Lower mountain snow depth: 66cm
Upper mountain snow depth: 86cm
Snow surface: Powder
5cm of new snow overnight, all the main trails have been groomed and there will be great skiing and riding to be had. There is plenty of nice lines off-piste, please be aware of the unmarked hazards off trail.
Weather
Sunny, Temperature: -0.8°C
It is currently snowing lightly with light to moderate South Easterly winds, visibility will be variable. Forecast for today is for continuing snow showers, light south easterly winds.
Is it time to book a lesson with the Snowsports School? A lesson will get you on top of your game and make every run more enjoyable. Don't Ski or Ride? Hit the Ozone tubing park from 10:30 today and stop in at the Ice Bar at the top of Alta Chairlift. See guest services for bookings.

Facilities Operating

Subject to conditions

All Lifts
Shadow Basin Quad
Open
Great skiing & riding within the basin on groomed trails. Plenty of off piste skiing to be had. Please watch for unmarked hazards off piste.
Sugar Bowl Quad
Open
Casterway trail open top to bottom for those easy graceful turns.
Alta Chair
Open
Alta Green trail is looking good for those wide connecting turns. Turquoise opens more trail options, and Alta Blue has the gates to practise your turns.
Base Lodge Conveyor
Open
The best place to commence your "on snow" experience. Easy access up the hill so you can concentrate on sliding downhill. Make sure to "High Five" the lift operator at the top.
Shorty Conveyor
Open
Just the right place for those first turns with a Starter Pack - lift ticket, gear and a lesson.
Pump House Conveyor
Open
Open for the learner skier/rider access.
Ozone Tubing Park
Open
Sun drenched and full of laughs! Bookings essential through guest services up the mountain. We tow you up...you slide down!

Terrain Parks
The Stash
Open
An awesome run through log and concrete features or just ski/ride to enjoy the trail. OPening out onto a big face looking back across the valley into Shadow Basin.
Dragon Big Park
Open
Intermediate line; side on down box, 15' jump, up cannon box, 15' jump, up-flat box, 15' jump, gap to/over/or hip Park Office. Expert line; JUMPS CLOSED TODAY. A side on down rail or down-flat-down rail, 12m jump, 10m jump, step up gap jump. Water race; a down-flat-down box or down bar, 25' jump, two hips, spine barrel donk, flat pipe. Frog Leg; gap down rail, closeout rail, wallride, down U rail and hike to the down rail. Gotham; side on down rail & step up hip with wall ride donk
TRIC Beginners Park
Open
We have now set up fun features down Castaway including Butter Box, two beginner jumps, an up box, up-down box, flat box, hip/table jump and step down jump.
