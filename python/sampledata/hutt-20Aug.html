<p><span>It is going to be a real ripper of a day at Mt Hutt! Bobs Knob and Platter Splatter are our pick of the day as they were groomed before the new snow arrived. Off-piste snow conditions remain variable as the new snow is a tad wind affected and sits on a firm, track-packed base. Nevertheless, powder pockets a plenty out there so come up and get into it</span></p>
